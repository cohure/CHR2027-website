CHR 2027

Seventh Conference on
Computational Humanities Research

The Computational Humanities Research (CHR) community is an international and interdisciplinary community that supports researchers with an interest in computational approaches to the humanities.

The 7th edition of the Computational Humanities Research conference will take place in Manchester, UK, from 5 to 8 January, 2027. The event will be organised by the Centre for Digital Humanities, Cultures and Media at the University of Manchester.
